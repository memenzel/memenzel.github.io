Biography

Hi! My name is Molly Menzel, welcome to my personal page. I study our planet’s atmosphere by using both idealized and fully comprehensive climate models. These models present valuable insight and allow us to further develop theory that describes past, present, and future climates. I am a doctoral student at Johns Hopkins University’s Department of Earth and Planetary Science working with Prof. Darryn Waugh. Currently, I’ve been focused on developing a better understanding of the relationship between the subtropical jet and Hadley Cell, and how that impacts both the tropics and the midlatitudes.
